COUNTY PROJECTS 2024;

RUIRU-GITHUNGURI ROAD

The road was viewed as one of the most used roads in Kiambu County;where it connects Ruiru and Githunguri and still links both sub-counties to Kiambu Town. As proposed by the county Governor Kimani Wa Matangi,the road will be re-constarcted and become a modern road.

By constracting this road many farmers around the area will be able to transport their producrs to the local milk industry and also to various retail shops.

CONSTRUCTION OF THE RUIRU DAM

As it was directed by the president,the county government allocated money for starting this big project. The National Government also allocated money for the same.By the construction of the dam,the county will be able to have a source of water by its self and this will reduce the cost of buying water from other resources. The dam is to come to a complition by the end of the year.5.7B Shillings have been allocated for the full contstruction of the dam. Here is an image of the dam site;

MODERN MARKETS PROJECT

Among the Governor's manifesto there was modern market building which is one of the projects that are very essential to the county. Through the years,we have been having the issue of roadside selling and by this project,the road side sellers will be able to have a condusive place to continue with their hustle and hopefully be able to expand their businesses.

The markets shall be built in the following Sub-Counties;

  1. RUIRU
  2. GITHUNGURI
  3. TATU CITY
  4. JUJA
Below is an image of the ongoing construction of the modern market in Ruiru.

These are the main projects that the county government have stated but still there are many other projects coming. The governor's aim is to make Kiambu county very developed and people living in Kiambu feel comfortable and live in good conditions.
